Lasagna

Mouth Watering Lasagna that the whole family will love

This recipe will have your family begging for lasagna night!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Lasagna Noodles (16 Ounces)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese (4 ounces)

Directions

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Pour the sauce
  3. Make the pasta
  4. Preheat the oven
  5. Combine ingredients
  6. Bake for 30 mins
  7. Remove from oven and enjoy
